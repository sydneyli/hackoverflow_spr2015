WHAT IS HACKOVERFLOW?

WiCS annual hackathon, HackOverflow, will take place on April 25 from 10am to 10pm. This year's theme is hardware, and participants will have access to everything from Oculus Rifts to Pebble watches! Through the event, we hope to encourage beginning programmers to participate in hackathons and to experience the practical applications of CS. But you don't need to be female or a beginner to participate! Anyone interested in hardware, programming, or just hackathons in general is welcome.
